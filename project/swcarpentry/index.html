Software Carpentry Mentor & Instructor

 

Mentoring Subcommitte

I am the co-chair of the mentoring subcommittee and an instructor for the Software Carpentry organization. Software Carpentry’s mission is to increase programming and bioinformatics literacy through 2-day workshops which cover various programming languages such as unix,git, R, python, SQL, and matlab.

As co-chair of the mentoring subcommittee, I host monthly subcommittee meetings to try to find ways new ways to expand ways to support, mentor, and create a network of instructors across the globe. In addition, I host instructor discussions with soon-to-be instructors and instructors who will teach or have recently taught a workshop. These discussions are a great way to meet other instructors across the globe, learn about challenges when teaching workshops (and how to prepare or fix them), and to learn new pedagogies for teaching bioinformatics.

More details on the mentoring subcommittee can be found here.
